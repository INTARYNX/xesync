Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 8, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data XEsync collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

  • Account credentials (username and password) to authenticate you in the app.
  • Bluetooth connection data to pair with your Xebex rowing machine.
  • Workout data: distance, duration, strokes, pace, power, calories, and heart rate.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your workout statistics and session history.
  • To sync your workout data to your account when connected to the internet.
  • To maintain a stable Bluetooth connection with your Xebex rower.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your workout data is stored on our servers solely for your own access.

Data Storage

Workout data is stored securely on servers operated by Fournier Digital Services. When offline, data is saved locally on your device until a connection is available.

Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take all practical steps to safeguard your information.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at info@fournier-digital.ch or visit fournier-digital.ch.
